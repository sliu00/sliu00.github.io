About Me...

My name is Samantha (Sam) Liu and I am a junior at Tufts University. I am double majoring in Computer Science and Child Study/Human Development with a concentration in 21st century literacies: media and technology. I am particularly interested in the intersection of children development, education, and technology, such as innovative and creative EdTech programs and applications. I have special interest in web development and software development. I have experience with C++, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS but I am always learning new skills everyday!

Beyond my studies, I have a passion for creativity and design. I love to experiment with photography and film, especially through portraits and travel videography. On the side, I also create jewlery pieces using polymer clay and resin.
